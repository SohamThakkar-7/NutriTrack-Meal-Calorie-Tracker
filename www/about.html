About NutriTrack

Our Purpose

Why NutriTrack?

NutriTrack was developed with the goal of creating a clean, intuitive tool for daily nutrition management. Many people struggle to maintain awareness of their caloric and macronutrient intake, which can affect health and energy levels significantly.

"Good nutrition is one of the keys to a healthy life. Tracking what you eat is the first step to understanding your relationship with food."

Our tracker provides a simple, privacy-first approach — all data is stored locally in the user's browser with no accounts sent to external servers, making it fast, secure, and accessible.
